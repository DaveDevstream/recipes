make a dough from flour, yeast, water, salt and sugar.
let it rise in a refrigerated container for 24 hours.
get a 16" pizza dish.
Flatten the dough into a circle using made pizza spinning skills.
put the dough on the dish.
Brush the dough with garlic butter.
slice into strips using a pizza cutter.
put cling film over the dough and pizza dish.
leave on top of hot oven for about an hour.
take clingfilm off and stick it in an oven on 350f for half and hour.
enjoy.
